## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, from ensuring basic functionality to tracking user behavior.

## Cookies We Use

This website uses only **strictly necessary cookies**. We do not use any analytics, marketing, or preference cookies.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `_csrf_token` | Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks. Required for secure operation of the website. | Session (deleted when browser is closed) |

## Legal Basis

Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent under Section 25(2) of the German Telecommunications Telemedia Data Protection Act (TTDSG) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR, as they are essential for the functioning of the website.

## Third-Party Cookies

We do not set any third-party cookies. No external services are loaded that could place cookies on your device.

## Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser to reject cookies. However, disabling the CSRF cookie may prevent some website features from working correctly.
